Web Development Final Project

This is my final project for the course. It is an application for users who are trying to be more health conscious. Users looking to improve their health can register as a client. Once they register, they will be navigated to their profile page where they can enter details about their fitness goals. They can search for recipes and view more details about the recipe. They can also comment and favorite the recipes. They can also search for trainers who post different workout exercises. They can follow the trainers so that they can go back to view the workouts later. Fitness professionals can register as trainers. Once they register, they will be navigated to a trainer profile page. They have the ability to post various workouts. They can also view who is following them.
